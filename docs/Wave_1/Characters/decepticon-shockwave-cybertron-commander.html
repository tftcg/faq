Wave 1 Characters

Decepticon Shockwave - Cybertron Commander

  1. I have a question about Wave 1 Shockwave’s bot mode ability. If I cause my opponent to scrap several cards at once (like with Fog of War), do they take 1 damage per card they scrap of just 1 damage in total.
    1 damage per card scrapped.
  2. I was playing with Bombshell - Decepticon Psy Ops with 5 health left, against Shockwave -Cybertron Commander. I scrapped 11 cards to KO him, but should his bot mode ability have triggered, killing me in the process?
    Shockwave’s ability will trigger 11 times, but if those are the last two characters on the battlefield, your opponent will lose the game because Shockwave has been KO’d by Bombshell’s ability, but Bombshell is not yet KO’d by Shockwave’s ability.

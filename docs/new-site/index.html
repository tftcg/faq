TRANSFORMERS TCG LIVES ON

On the 20th of July 2020, Wizards of the Coast announced that they were no longer going to create any more releases, citing not enough growth into the broader Transformers fan base, along with COVID-19 difficulties.

That did not stop the growing community of players who called the game their own. They have continued to play the game, they have continued to host tournaments, they have continued to create content for each other, and they have started to determine their own rules and to create new community cards.

The game continues...

TITAN MASTERS ATTACKS!

Titan Masters Attack is released on SOMEDAY.

Customize Transformers characters with Titan Masters Attack! Mix and match Body and Head character cards to create a character which matches your play style.

Each Titan Masters Attack Booster Pack contains either TWO foil Transformers Character Cards - 1 small head and 1 large body OR 1 large Character card and 1 Stratagem card.

War for Cybertron Siege II

The War for Cybertron reaches its explosive conclusion in War for Cybertron: Siege II! Megatron and the Decepticons have conquered the planet. Optimus Prime desperately leads surviving Autobots to escape in the Ark. And both sides turn to Mercenaries to fill out their ranks in the final battles.

Each War for Cybertron: Siege II TCG Booster Pack contains TWO foil Transformers Character Cards - 1 large and 1 small. Small Character Cards are the size of a battle card.

War for Cybertron Siege I

The War for Cybertron: Siege 1 begins - choose a side - Autobot or Decepticon! War for Cybertron: Siege I introduces small character cards: Battle Masters and Micro Masters.

Each War for Cybertron: Siege TCG Booster Pack contains TWO foil Transformers Character Cards - 1 large and 1 small. Small Character Cards are the size of a battle card.

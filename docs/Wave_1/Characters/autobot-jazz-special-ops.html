TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Autobot Jazz, Special Ops

  1. If the attacking character would deal more than 2 damage, what happens to the extra damage?
    The extra damage doesn’t happen. The attacking character will deal only 2 damage.
  2. Let’s say Autobot Jazz defends and the attacker has an ability that deals damage to him. Does that damage count for Autobot Jazz’s ability?
    Yes. Any damage dealt to Autobot Jazz while he’s defending and his ability is active will count. If the attacker has an ability that deals 1 damage to Autobot Jazz, then he can’t take more than 1 more damage from the attack.

Wave 2 Battle Cards

Cornered!

  1. When I play Cornered! and force my opponent to flip one of their character's do they get to use a "when you flip to this mode” Ability on my turn?
    Yes. If you play Cornered! on an opponent’s character, they get to use a flip ability on that turn.

Wave 1 Characters

Bumblebee, Legendary Warrior

  1. Does flipping Bumblebee to alt mode mean I have to attack with him that turn?
    No. It just lets Bumblebee attack untapped enemies if you decide to attack with Bumblebee.
  2. Can Bumblebee still attack tapped enemies that turn?
    Yes. Because Bumblebee will be able to attack any enemy, tapped or untapped, you choose which enemy he attacks.
  3. Does attacking an untapped enemy cause that enemy to become tapped?
    No. The enemy will stay untapped. None of the other rules of combat change.

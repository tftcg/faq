Getting Started with Transformers TCG

Hasbro's How to Play guides are a good way to get into the game, as are early content like Team Covenant's How to Play and Wossy Play's How to Play videos.

The Rules

The Community elected a ruling committee, Aequitas, to handle any rule questions. Aequitas provide a set of rules documents and rulings via the TF Rules website.

FAQ

Official rulings are available through a FAQ Database, and periodically through Rules Roundups

Getting Help

As you get into a card game, you will find you understand the basic rules, but are unsure on a specific interaction. Online communities are excellent places to seek help. We recommend joining a Discord channel, Facebook group, or asking on Reddit. Here are links to one of each:

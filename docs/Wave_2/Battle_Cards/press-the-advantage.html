Wave 2 Battle Cards

Press the Advantage

  1. Can I play this if I don’t have any Autobots or my opponent doesn’t have any Decepticons just to get one of the effects?
    Yes.
  2. Let’s say I play this and choose my opponent’s Decepticon with 1 defense. Later in the turn, it gets +2 defense. What will its total defense be?
    1. Press the Advantage won’t drop the Decepticon’s defense below 0, but the -2 applies again once the Decepticon’s defense is raised.

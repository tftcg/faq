TFTCG FAQ
Wave 2 Characters

Blurr - Fastest Bot in the Galaxy

  1. If my opponent has multiple untapped characters when I use Blurr’s ability, which one does it attack?
    You choose, just as you would for any battle.
  2. Can I play any cards in before it attacks the untapped character?
    No. The second attack starts right after the first one finishes.
  3. Do I have to attack a second time, or can I just leave Blurr untapped?
    You do. Note that if there isn’t an untapped enemy, the ability doesn’t trigger and Blurr won’t untap.

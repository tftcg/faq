TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Ransack, Insecticon Commando

  1. What happens if I flip Ransack to alt mode and there are no damaged characters?
    In that case, nothing happens. No damage counters are put on Ransack.
  2. What happens if the only damaged characters are enemies? Can I choose one of my characters and do nothing?
    Yes. You choose any other character, damaged or not. Then move 2 damage counters from that character onto Ransack fi you can.
  3. How does Ransack’s ability work with Actions or Upgrades that raise his attack?
    Ransack’s ability sets his base attack value, what you’d see printed on the card if it didn’t change. From there, Actions and Upgrades can raise that value. For example, if Ransack has 5 damage counters, his base attack would be 5. If he then got a Weapon that gave him +2 attack, his attack would be 7. This wouldn’t change the number of damage counters on him.

TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Slipstream, Strategic Seeker

  1. What happens if the 1 damage counter I move onto the defender is enough to KO it?
    If either character is KO’d in the middle of an attack, the attack immediately ends. In that case, you won’t flip battle cards (and neither will your opponent).
  2. What does “3 different battle icons” mean? If I flip two orange battle icons and one blue battle icon, does that count?
    No. It means you must flip at least one of three different battle icons. For example, one orange, one blue, and one white battle icon.

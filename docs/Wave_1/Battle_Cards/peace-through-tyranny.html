TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Battle Cards

Peace through Tyranny

  1. Let’s say I play Peace through Tyranny and end my turn with no untapped characters, while at least one of my opponent’s characters is untapped. What happens during my extra turn? Can I attack?
    Not unless you find a way to untap one of your characters.
  2. What happens if I somehow play two of these in the same turn?
    In that case, you’ll take two extra turns after the current turn.

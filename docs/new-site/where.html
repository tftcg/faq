Playing Over Webcam

One way to play Transformers that is currently popular is to play via Webcams. There is a Facebook Group to help get setup and arrange games. One popular approach, if you don't own a Webcam, is to use DroidCam on a phone (Android, iPhone), that connects over WiFi to your computer, and then you use a channel like Discord to share video and chat with your opponent.

OCTGN

Another way to play Transformers it via OCTGN. OCTGN is a Windows application that can support various card games via installed gamepacks. One of those is Transformers. To install:

  1. Install the app from, and create your account at: octgn.net/Home/GetOctgn
  2. Open OCTGN, and go to "Game Manager" to install "Transformers"
  3. Open the Deck Builder, and use the Plugins→Image Downloader to download the card images
  4. Then you can build your deck. You can also download a deck directly from fortressmaximus.io using the "OCTGN Export" function.

The following announcement for a competition at TFcon also has a how to install and play video.

Online Schedule

Playing in Store

While the COVID-19 Pandemic has forced us online, local game stores are reopening and a great place to buy cards and play games. If your local gaming group is large enough, they are likely to even be prepared to host competitions. If you search on Facebook, there are many regional groups dedicated to playing Transformers.

Conventions

Through 2019, Transformers was played at GenCon, Origins, and then at the Energon Invitiational at Pax Unplugged. A virtual tournament will be held at TFcon in October 2020. Hopefully there will be many more conference tournaments.

Creating Custom Cards

While you may wish to design your cards using a tool like Photoshop or Krita, Edge of Iacon have produced an excellent template for the Magic Set Editor that allows anyone to easily make a custom card.

You can find that on the The Edge of Iacon Download page.

Printing Custom Cards

Most custom card creators are providing PDFs to print.

TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Autobot Hound, Long-Range Scout

  1. Let’s say you attack with another character and flip a white battle icon. Then, because your opponent’s characters are all tapped, you get to attack again. For your second attack, you attack with Autobot Hound and flip a white battle icon. Will his ability trigger?
    No. The white battle icon you flipped while attacking with Autobot Hound wasn’t your first white battle icon that turn, so he won’t get the bonus.

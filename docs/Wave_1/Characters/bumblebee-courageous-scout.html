Wave 1 Characters

Bumblebee, Courageous Scout

  1. What happens if I have no cards in my hand when I use this ability?
    You’ll draw a card, look at it, and immediately put it on top of your deck. You can’t do anything else with the card (like play it) before putting it on top of your deck.
  2. Let’s say I reveal one white battle icon during Bumblebee’s combat. Then I flip another white battle icon in the two bonus cards. Will his ability trigger?
    Yes. For any ability that looks at what battle icons you flipped, flip your two bonus cards before checking if that ability triggers or not.
